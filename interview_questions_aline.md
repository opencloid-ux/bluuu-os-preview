# Interview Questions — Aline (OTA Manager)
## Bluuu Tours — BLUUU OS Project

---

## Why We're Doing This

We're building **BLUUU OS** — an AI-powered operations system for Bluuu Tours. The goal is to automate the most repetitive, manual tasks so that you and the team can focus on what actually matters.

Before we build anything, we need to understand exactly how things work right now — the real process, not the ideal one. There are no wrong answers. The more honest and detailed you are, the better the system we can build for you.

**This interview will take about 20–30 minutes.** You can record a voice note, write your answers, or do a voice call — whatever is easiest for you.

Everything you share stays within the team and is used only to improve our internal systems.

---

## Questions

### Your Role
1. In your own words, what is your job at Bluuu? What does a typical day look like from morning to evening?
2. Who do you communicate with the most during the day — and about what?

### OTA Availability Management
3. Which OTA platforms do you manage? (Viator, Klook, GetYourGuide, Airbnb, TripAdvisor — which ones and how many?)
4. Walk me through exactly what happens when someone sends you "please close" for a date. Step by step — what do you do?
5. How many times per day do you receive requests to open or close availability? On a busy day vs. a slow day?
6. How long does it take you to close one date across all platforms?
7. Do you ever make mistakes — open when you should close, or miss a platform? What happens when that occurs?
8. Is there a system or spreadsheet you use to track what's open/closed? Or is it all manual checking?
9. What is the most frustrating part of managing availability?

### Bookings & Coordination
10. When a booking comes in through an OTA, what do you do with that information? Who gets notified?
11. How do you know when a tour is full? Does the system tell you, or do you check manually?
12. What happens when there's an overbooking? How often does it happen?
13. Do you ever need to move bookings between boats or dates? How do you handle that?

### Communication
14. How do you receive instructions — WhatsApp, Odoo, verbal? Is there a standard process or is it different every time?
15. If Karim or Dima changes the availability rules (e.g., "only 10 seats max on this boat"), how does that information reach you?

### Problems & Pain Points
16. What is the one thing you do every day that you wish a computer could do for you?
17. What is the most common mistake or problem that happens with OTA management?
18. Have you ever had a situation where a guest showed up but there was no space on the boat? What happened?

### Ideal Future
19. If you had a magic button that could do one thing automatically, what would it be?
20. What would make your job significantly easier?
