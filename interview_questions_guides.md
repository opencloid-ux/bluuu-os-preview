# Interview Questions — Tour Guides
## Bluuu Tours — BLUUU OS Project

---

## Why We're Doing This

We're building **BLUUU OS** — a system to make operations smoother for everyone at Bluuu. We want to automate the boring, repetitive stuff so you can focus on what you do best: creating amazing experiences for guests.

Before we build anything, we need to hear from you directly — not your manager, not the office. **You.** Because you're the one actually on the boat every day.

There are no wrong answers. The more honest you are, the better the system we build for you.

**Record a voice note — 15 to 20 minutes is enough.** You don't need to answer every question or go in order. Just talk about how things actually work.

Everything you share stays within the team.

---

## Questions

### Your Daily Routine
1. What time do you usually arrive at the harbor? What's the first thing you do?
2. How do you find out which boat you're on and what type of tour it is?
3. How do you receive your guest list for the day? Is it always clear and complete?
4. What information do you wish you had before the trip that you often don't get?

### Guests & Communication
5. How do you know if a guest has a dietary restriction, allergy, or special need? Is this information always passed to you?
6. What are the most common questions guests ask you during the trip?
7. What guest situations are hardest to handle? (Language barrier, medical issue, unhappy guest, etc.)
8. How do you communicate with the lounge team or office during the trip if something goes wrong?

### On the Boat
9. Walk me through what you do from the moment guests board to the moment they leave the boat.
10. What is your role during snorkeling? How do you manage different swimming levels?
11. What happens if a guest gets seasick, injured, or has a medical emergency?
12. How do you decide what extra information to share at each snorkeling spot?

### Problems & Emergencies
13. What is the most stressful situation you've experienced on a tour? How did you handle it?
14. What happens if the boat breaks down or there's bad weather during the trip?
15. Have you ever had to make a decision on your own without being able to contact the office? What was it?

### Feedback & Improvement
16. After a tour, how do you report back to Geebran or the office? Is there a formal process?
17. Do you ever read your own reviews? How does it feel when guests mention you by name?
18. What do you wish Bluuu management understood better about your job?
19. What is the one thing that would make your job easier every day?
20. If you could change one thing about how Bluuu operates, what would it be?

Record a voice note on your phone and send it to Geebran or Alex. You can answer in English or Bahasa Indonesia — whatever feels natural.

**Thank you. Your input will directly shape how we build the system.**
