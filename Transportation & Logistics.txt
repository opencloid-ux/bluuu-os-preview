Timing & Schedule
[вопрос] - What time is pickup from our hotel/resort? 

It depends on your location. Our driver will contact you a day before the trip to confirm the pickup time and location.
 
[вопрос] - What time does the boat depart?  

Shared tours depart at 8 AM, premium shared tours at 8:30 AM, and private tours depart at different times between 9 and 10 AM depending on the boat you choose. You can check the itinerary before booking on our website and pick the one that suits you best.

[вопрос] - Is it possible to start an hour later/earlier?  

If you book a private tour, you can depart later, but it may require slightly adjusting the itinerary or skipping a snorkeling spot. It’s not possible to depart earlier due to the harbor schedule. Shared tours depart at fixed times and cannot be changed.

Special Requests & Needs
[вопрос] - Can pregnant guests join?

Pregnant guests can join the tour; however, if the pregnancy is 26 weeks or more, it’s not recommended for safety reasons.

[вопрос] - Do drivers speak specific languages (e.g., English, French)? 

All our drivers speak both Indonesian and English :)
 
[вопрос] - Will the boat wait if we’re late?  

If you book a shared tour, the departure time is fixed and unfortunately, the boat won’t be able to wait for late arrivals.
If you book a private tour, the boat will definitely wait for you :)

[вопрос] - How will a large group (e.g., 9, 16 people) be transferred?
