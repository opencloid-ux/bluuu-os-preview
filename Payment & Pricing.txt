# Cancellations & Refunds
[вопрос] - How will refunds be processed (PayPal/card/bank transfer)? 

We can issue a refund only through the same method you used to pay for the tour.
 
[вопрос] - Will I get email confirmation for refunds? 

We will notify you as soon as the refund has been issued.
 
[вопрос] - Are refunds issued for weather cancellations or emergencies? 

If we cancel the tours on the day of the trip due to bad weather, we will offer either a full refund or an alternative date. If you wish to cancel and receive a refund yourself, it must be done no later than 24 hours before the tour departure.
 
[вопрос] - Can I get a refund for illness?  

Cancellation with a refund due to illness is only possible no later than 24 hours before the tour departure.

[вопрос] - How do I check refund status?

From the moment we issue the refund, it may take a few days for the funds to appear in your account. We recommend contacting your bank for more detailed information.

[вопрос] - What if we cancel 24 hours before?  

You can cancel the trip and receive a refund up to 24 hours before the tour. Unfortunately, refunds are not possible if you cancel less than 24 hours before the boat's departure.
