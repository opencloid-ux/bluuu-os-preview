# Boat Features & Facilities
[вопрос] - Does the boat have a bathroom/toilet? How many bathrooms are there?

Yes, each boat is equipped with a small toilet.

[вопрос] - Is there a changing room/shower on board?

The boats are not equipped with a shower or changing room, but all of that is available at our new office, where you’re welcome to use the facilities.

[вопрос] - Is there air conditioning?  

Most boats are not equipped with air conditioning as they have an open cabin. However, if you require a closed cabin with air conditioning, we recommend booking a higher-class yacht such as the AR55, JA20, AZ50, or RZ50. You can find more options on our website.

[вопрос] - Are there tables for eating?  

Most boats are equipped with a small table where you can have a snack or place your drinks. Please check the boat photos on our website before booking to choose the model that suits you best.

# Equipment & Activities
[вопрос] - Are full-face snorkel masks available?  

Yes, of course! Full-face snorkeling masks are available. You can add them during the booking process or later in your personal account on our website.

[вопрос] - Is a GoPro/underwater camera provided? Is photography included? 

Underwater shots with a GoPro are included in all our tours. You’ll receive your content within three days after the trip.
 

# Safety & Accessibility
[вопрос] - Are life jackets provided for all ages? Are they mandatory for non-swimmers? 

Each boat is always equipped with a sufficient number of life jackets. For those who cannot swim, we always recommend using them during snorkeling.

[вопрос] - Can non-swimmers snorkel?  

Yes, of course! Non-swimmers can absolutely snorkel — our guides will be happy to assist and provide instructions. They’ll also stay close by during the snorkeling session. You’re also welcome to use life jackets to help you float while snorkeling.

[вопрос] - Are snorkeling spots safe?  

All snorkeling spots are not only beautiful but also safe. Our guides stay with guests at all times during the snorkeling, so there’s nothing to worry about.

[вопрос] - Are infants allowed? Are there regulations for them?  

Infants are allowed only on private tours (not on shared options). However, insurance coverage applies only to children aged 7 and above.

[вопрос] - Can we bring a baby? Is there shaded seating?

Infants are allowed only on private tours (not on shared options). However, insurance coverage applies only to children aged 7 and above. Shaded seats are available on all our boats.

# Food & Drinks
[вопрос] - Is lunch/snacks included? Are there vegan/vegetarian options?  

Lunch at a restaurant on Nusa Penida or Lembongan (depending on the tour type) is included. Vegetarian options are available at all restaurants; however, if you require a vegan meal, please let us know in advance so we can contact the restaurant to confirm availability.

[вопрос] - What drinks are provided (water, soft drinks, alcohol)?  

Drinking water is always available on the boat. If you'd like to add other beverages, you can do so during booking (for an additional charge), or you're welcome to bring your own. Please note that alcohol (such as liquor, whiskey, vodka, etc.) is only allowed on private tours, while only beer and non-alcoholic drinks are permitted on shared tours.

[вопрос] - Can we bring our own food/drinks/alcohol?  

Of course, you're welcome to bring your own food — just keep in mind that lunch is included in all tours :)

# Logistics & Policies
[вопрос] - Where is the departure port? Is parking available?  

Departure is from Serangan Harbor. We meet our guests at our office near the port, where public parking is also available for an additional fee — you can leave your car or scooter there.

Here’s the location 📍: https://maps.app.goo.gl/42XzKnyNLdmgcmAz7

[вопрос] - Can we store bags during the tour?

You can bring your bags on the boat, but please note that space is limited. If you're bringing a large amount of items or luggage, kindly let us know the size and weight in advance.
 
[вопрос] - Are there entrance fees for islands?  

All tickets and required admissions are included in our tour :)

[вопрос] - Can we decorate the boat for special occasions?  

Yes, we can help with decorations. Please let us know what exactly you need, and we’ll be happy to assist with the arrangements.

[вопрос] - Is motion sickness medicine available?  

Seasickness medicine **is** available on every boat. Our guides will offer it to you before the trip or at any time during the tour if needed.

[вопрос] - Is sunscreen provided?  

Sunscreen is not included in the tour, but you’re welcome to bring your own or purchase it at our office before the trip.

[вопрос] - Can we pay online? Is cash required?  

You can pay a 50% deposit online during booking and settle the remaining balance in cash or by card at our office. You’re also welcome to pay the full amount in advance to save time on the morning of your trip :)

# Special Requests
[вопрос] - Can we rent a GoPro?  

You can rent a GoPro, but it’s usually not necessary since underwater GoPro footage is already included in the tour. Our guides will be happy to assist you with photos and videos throughout the trip.

[вопрос] - Can we bring a drone?  

If you book a private tour, you're welcome to bring your own drone. Alternatively, you can book a drone pilot through us — this option is more convenient and safer, especially considering the sometimes challenging weather and wind conditions around Nusa Penida. With our drone pilot, you won’t need to worry or spend time filming yourself, and you'll receive your content within just three days after the trip.

[вопрос] - Can we add bean bags?  

Beanbags are already available on our boats — you can use them onboard or even float on them in the water :)

[вопрос] - Is jet ski/seabob available?

At the moment, we do not offer jet skis or seabobs.


# Miscellaneous
[вопрос] - Is there a guide?  

Of course, our guides always accompany guests throughout the trip on all of our tours.

[вопрос] - Can we visit beaches?  

At the moment, our boats cannot dock directly at the beaches, but we can arrange a land tour for you, where you'll be driven to them by car. Please check the list of available destinations on our website (such as Diamond Beach), and book the itinerary that suits you best.

[вопрос] - Is there a photographer?  

On standard shared tours, only underwater GoPro footage is available. On premium shared or premium private tours, a photographer is included. If you book a standard private tour, you can add a photographer for an additional fee. You can check the inclusions and also add a photographer either during the booking process or later through your personal account on our website.

[вопрос] - Are there changing facilities at the port?  

You’re welcome to use the changing facilities and shower at our office before and after the trip.

[вопрос] - What is the maximum group capacity?

The maximum number of guests on shared tours is 14 people. If you're booking a private tour, the capacity depends on the boat you choose. Please check the boats and their descriptions on our website.
