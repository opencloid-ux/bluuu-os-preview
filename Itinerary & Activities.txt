# Activities & Inclusions
[вопрос] - Is snorkeling included? How many spots?

Of course, snorkeling is included — it’s what we do best! :) Our standard tour includes four snorkeling spots, including swimming with the incredible manta rays. You can also explore other options on our website and choose the one that suits you best.

[вопрос] - Can we arrange a photographer/DJ?

Of course, you can book a photographer and a drone pilot during the booking process on our website or later through your personal account. At the moment, we don’t have a DJ on our team, so we’re unable to offer that service. Please also note that most boats are not equipped with power outlets, so setting up DJ equipment and sound systems is only possible on certain yachts. Let us know, and we’ll help you choose the right yachts for your event.

[вопрос] - Can we bring musical instruments/speakers?

We provide portable JBL speakers on our boats. If you'd like to bring musical instruments or additional sound equipment, please note that most boats are not equipped with power outlets, so setup is only possible on certain yachts. Let us know, and we’ll help you choose the right yachts for your event.

[вопрос] - Are inflatables/paddle boards included?

At the moment, we do not provide inflatables or paddle boards.

[вопрос] - Is there free parking?

There is a public parking area near our office. Please note that it is not free and not included in the tour.

[вопрос] - Is insurance included?

Of course, insurance is included in the trip for guests aged 8 to 70 years old.

[вопрос] - Is canoeing/water sports available?

Unfortunately, at the moment we do not offer canoeing or water sports.

[вопрос] - What other activities besides snorkeling?

In addition to snorkeling, we can also offer a land tour or simply a relaxing cruise on the boat, enjoying stunning views and chilling along the way.

[вопрос] - Will we meet manta rays while snorkeling?

There’s a high chance of seeing manta rays during snorkeling, as we carefully select the best spots for this experience. However, we can’t guarantee it 100%, as they are wild creatures and occasionally choose different routes.

[вопрос] - Is seeing turtles guaranteed?

There’s a high chance of seeing turtles during snorkeling, as we carefully select the best spots for this experience. However, we can’t guarantee it 100%, as they are wild creatures and occasionally choose different routes.

[вопрос] - Is Manta Point or Manta Bay better for mantas?

Both of these spots are great for swimming with manta rays, but since we can’t predict in advance where they will appear at a specific time, we usually visit Manta Bay first — and if there are no mantas there, we’ll head to Manta Point.

[вопрос] - Can we shower after snorkeling?

Of course, you’re welcome to use the shower and change clothes after the tour at our office.

[вопрос] - Is there an English-speaking guide?

Of course — all of our guides speak English.

# Safety & Accessibility
[вопрос] - Are there dangerous marine life (stingrays, crocodiles)?

We don’t encounter any marine creatures that are dangerous to humans during our trips.

[вопрос] - Is there walking involved?

It depends on the itinerary you choose. In the standard itinerary, for example, we visit Kelingking Cliff, where you only need to walk a short distance from the parking area. However, if you select a full-day land tour, there will be more walking involved — though mostly just short walks from the car to each attraction.

[вопрос] - What happens if we get tired mid-tour?

If you get too tired and decide to end the trip early, and you booked a shared tour, our guide will assist you in purchasing a ticket for a public speedboat operated by another company so you can return to Bali. If you booked a private tour, you’ll have the flexibility to return to the departure point at any time. Please note, however, that in such cases we unfortunately won’t be able to offer a refund.
