# Safety & Swimming Ability
[вопрос] - Is snorkeling safe for kids, elderly (77+), or injured individuals?  

Snorkeling is absolutely safe for children!
Unfortunately, guests with any kind of injury won’t be able to join the tour for safety reasons.
Guests up to 70 years old can join the tour.

[вопрос] - Are currents/waves strong? Is drift snorkeling safe?  

Underwater currents can be strong at some spots, but there’s nothing to worry about — our guides will be with you during the snorkeling, and the boat will stay nearby as well.

# Wildlife & Snorkeling Spots
[вопрос] - Will we see manta rays, turtles, or sharks? Is it guaranteed? 

The chances of seeing mantas and turtles are very, very high — we spot them almost every day.
However, we can’t guarantee their appearance 100%, as they are wild marine creatures and might have their own plans for the day :)

[вопрос] - Where are the snorkeling spots (e.g., Manta Bay, Crystal Bay, Mangrove)?  

For our tour, we’ve selected the best snorkeling spots: Bali Hai, Wall Point, SD Point, and either Manta Bay or Manta Point — depending on where there’s a higher chance of seeing mantas on the day of the trip.

[вопрос] - What marine life can we expect at each spot?  

At each spot, you can see fish, turtles, and even dolphins. At Manta Bay or Manta Point, you may get the chance to see amazing manta rays. However, we can’t guarantee their appearance 100%, as they are wild marine creatures and might have their own plans for the day :)

# Weather & Conditions
[вопрос] - Are high waves common at snorkel sites?

Sea conditions vary across different snorkeling spots and also depend on the weather and wind on the specific day, so it’s not possible to predict the swell in advance.
 
[вопрос] - Is training provided for beginners?

We don’t provide training for snorkeling, but our guides give a safety briefing before the trip and additional briefings before each snorkeling spot.
However, if you’d like to book a diving tour, we can provide full training with a professional dive instructor.
