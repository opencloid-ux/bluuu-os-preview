[вопрос] - Can we choose dishes/pre-order/customize?  

Since the restaurants have created set menus specifically for our guests, at the moment you can only choose from the dishes included in our menu. However, if you prefer to order a la carte, you’re welcome to choose any additional dishes or drinks for an extra charge.

[вопрос] - Can we skip the included lunch?  

You can skip lunch if you prefer, but we recommend bringing some snacks with you, as snorkeling requires physical effort and the tour lasts around 8 hours — you’ll definitely need some energy :)

[вопрос] - What time is lunch?

Lunch time depends on the type of tour you choose. Please check our itineraries before booking to select the option that suits you best. The lunch break typically lasts around one hour.

## Venue Logistics:  
[вопрос] - Can we choose the lunch restaurant?  

The lunch spot is pre-selected to ensure the most efficient itinerary, allowing you to visit all the planned spots. If you’ve booked a private tour, you’re welcome to let us know where you’d prefer to have lunch, and we’ll do our best to arrange it for you. Please note that lunch is only included at the locations listed on our website — if you choose to dine elsewhere, the lunch will be at your own expense, and it may require changes to the itinerary, possibly skipping one or more snorkeling spots.


[вопрос] - Do restaurants accept credit cards, or is cash needed?

Most restaurants do accept cards, but we recommend bringing enough cash with you just in case — for example, in the event of a power outage or internet issues.

[вопрос] - Are vegetarian/vegan/gluten-free options available?  

Vegetarian options are available at all restaurants. However, if you require vegan or gluten-free meals, please let us know in advance so we can check with the restaurant if it’s possible to arrange.

# Activities & Miscellaneous
## Activities:  
[вопрос] - What are sea conditions like?  

Sea conditions change very quickly in Bali and Nusa Penida, so an accurate forecast is only available on the day of the trip.

[вопрос] - What about jellyfish?

The appearance of jellyfish is unpredictable as it depends on the weather and currents. If we spot jellyfish at one of the locations, snorkeling there will not be possible and we’ll head to the next safe spot instead.

## Accessibility & Discounts:  
[вопрос] - Do you offer discounts for children?  

Children under 5 years old can join private tours free of charge, while children aged 6 and above will need to be paid for at the adult rate. For shared tours, only children aged 8 and above are allowed.

[вопрос] - Is breakfast available near the departure point?

You’ll be able to order breakfast at our office, and we also offer complimentary tea or coffee :)
