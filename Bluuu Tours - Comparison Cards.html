★ Ideas to Make First Class Truly Elite

Proposed upgrades to differentiate First Class from Premium and justify the price gap

🎥 Drone Video Package
30-second cinematic drone video of the yacht + guests + ocean. Edited and sent to WhatsApp same day. Guests will 100% post this on Instagram/TikTok.
Cost: ~$15/trip (drone operator already on boat)
🏇 GoPro Rental
Waterproof GoPro available for the day. All underwater manta footage goes home with the guest. Branded Bluuu memory card sleeve.
Cost: ~$5/trip (already own GoPros)
🍴 Premium Picnic Box
Instead of standard lunch, a curated picnic box with Balinese wraps, tropical fruit, cold brew coffee, homemade cookies. Presented beautifully = more photos.
Cost: ~$8-12/person extra vs standard lunch
💫 Branded Welcome Kit
Waterproof phone pouch + Bluuu bracelet with Nusa Penida coordinates + reef-safe sunscreen sample. Guests keep it all = walking advertising.
Cost: ~$3-5/person (bulk order from Alibaba)
🎆 Sunset Prosecco Ceremony
Not just "a glass of prosecco" — a proper ceremony: captain slows the boat, music changes to chill playlist, crew pours prosecco with a fruit platter. Make it a moment.
Cost: ~$3/person extra (presentation, not product)
📷 Photo Stop at Secret Spot
First Class only: 10-minute stop at a photogenic cliff/cave that other boats skip. Exclusive to Eldorado = "you can't get this anywhere else."
Cost: $0 (just route adjustment + fuel)
🎵 Captain's Playlist
JBL speaker on Eldorado. Before the trip, ask guests for their Spotify playlist link. Personal touch that costs nothing but feels premium.
Cost: $0
💌 Bean Bags on Sun Deck
Waterproof bean bags on the Eldorado sun deck. Guests lounge while cruising. No other operator has this. Extremely Instagrammable.
Cost: ~$50-80 one-time (marine-grade bean bags)
