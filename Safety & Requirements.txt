# General Safety & Risk
[вопрос] - Is it safe?

All our tours are absolutely safe.

[вопрос] - Will we be safe with kids?

Yes, absolutely, it’s safe with kids — our guides will keep an eye on them, but we highly recommend that parents also watch over their children during the trip :)

[вопрос] - What happens in emergencies?

In case of an emergency or injury, our team will provide first aid and, if needed, assist you in getting to a hospital. Every guest aged 8 to 70 is insured with coverage of up to 200,000,000 IDR.

[вопрос] - What if an accident happens?

In case of an emergency or injury, our team will provide first aid and, if needed, assist you in getting to a hospital. Every guest aged 8 to 70 is insured with coverage of up to 200,000,000 IDR.

[вопрос] - What if someone gets sick?

If someone feels unwell during the trip, we have a first aid kit with basic medications. If you decide not to continue the tour: for shared tour guests, we can help arrange a public boat back to Bali (tickets are paid separately at the counter of the boat company you choose), and for private tours, the boat can return to Bali at any time upon your request.

[вопрос] - Are there any shark risks?

The chances of encountering a shark are extremely low. Moreover, the types of sharks that are occasionally seen in the waters around Nusa Penida are harmless to humans — they are not aggressive.
And of course, our snorkeling spots are located in safe areas where spotting a shark is considered a rare and lucky experience, not a threat :)
