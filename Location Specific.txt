Correct, Kelingking is located on the west (southwestern) coast of Nusa Penida.

[вопрос] Is Manta Point crowded?

Manta Point is usually crowded in the morning, so we take our guests there in the afternoon to avoid tourist crowds and create a more relaxed and personal experience.

[вопрос] Is it possible to feed the mantas?

Manta rays, just like any other marine creatures, must never be touched or fed under any circumstances. We always follow respectful and responsible snorkeling practices to protect the ocean and its inhabitants.

[вопрос] Is snorkeling with Manta safe?

Snorkeling with manta rays is absolutely safe — they are completely harmless to humans. They're gentle, curious creatures, and swimming near them is an unforgettable experience!
