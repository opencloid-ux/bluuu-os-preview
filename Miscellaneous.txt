# Cancellation, Changes & Flexibility
[вопрос] - Can we leave early or extend the time?

Early departure is not possible, but if you book a private tour, you can choose the one-hour extension to simply relax on the boat out at sea near the harbor.

[вопрос] - Is it possible to change from full-day to half-day?

If you book a private tour, you can shorten the duration, but please note that you’ll likely have to skip most of the snorkeling spots and possibly even lunch. The boat ride to and from Nusa Penida can take up to 2 hours, so we highly recommend going for the full-day experience.

[вопрос] - Can we cancel or get a refund?

You can cancel your trip and receive a full refund anytime up to 24 hours before the boat’s departure. Please make sure to review the cancellation policy during the booking process on our website.

[вопрос] - How does the refund process work?

If you booked through our website, the refund will be processed immediately by our accounting team. Please note that it may take a few days for the funds to appear in your account, depending on your bank.

[вопрос] - Can we modify the tour (e.g., skip land, change lunch)?

If you book a private tour, you can request some changes to your itinerary, but we can't guarantee major modifications. Just let us know what you'd like to adjust, and we’ll check if it’s possible.

[вопрос] - How flexible is the tour timing?

The timing on shared tours is fixed. If you book a private tour, you can adjust the schedule slightly—just let us know what you'd like to change, and we’ll check if it’s possible.

[вопрос] - What is the refund policy for transfers?

If the tour was cancelled but you already used the transfers, unfortunately we won’t be able to issue a refund.

[вопрос] - Can we get a boat last-minute (e.g., tomorrow)?

Yes, of course! You can book a tour for tomorrow as long as it's before 9:00 PM Bali time and if there’s availability.

[вопрос] - What is the process if I need to cancel?

If you book a tour on our website, you’ll be able to cancel it in your personal account no later than 24 hours before the boat’s departure. Please review the cancellation policy during the booking process on our website.

All our crew members have received professional training in diving, snorkeling, and first aid, and all our captains are licensed by the Indonesian Ministry of Transportation.
