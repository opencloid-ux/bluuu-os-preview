FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How to book your Nusa Penida tour?+

Choose your tour type, select your date and group size, then complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation email with all details including pickup time and meeting point at Sanur port.

Why are we more expensive than other companies?+

We invest in newer boats (2024–2026 fleet), professional guides with 3+ years experience, and include all-inclusive meals and snorkeling equipment. Our 4.9 rating across 10,660 reviews speaks for the quality difference.

Manta Bay or Manta Point?+

Both are great — Manta Bay (Nusa Penida north side) is calmer and better for beginners. Manta Point (south side) has larger mantas but stronger currents. Your guide will recommend based on conditions that day.

Private or group Nusa Penida tour?+

Groups of 6+ usually save money with a private charter. Shared tours are great for solo travelers and couples. Private gives you a custom schedule and the whole boat to yourselves.

How far is Nusa Penida from Bali?+

About 45 minutes by speedboat from Sanur port in Bali. We depart at 08:30 and return around 17:00 — a full day on the water.
