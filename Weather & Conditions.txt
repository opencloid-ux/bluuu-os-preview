Weather & Tour Operation:
[вопрос] - How does rain affect snorkeling visibility and the tour?

Rain usually doesn’t affect snorkeling, but if the weather conditions aren’t suitable for snorkeling, our guides may adjust the itinerary to ensure the best and safest experience.

[вопрос] - How do sea conditions (waves, choppiness, swell) impact the tour and cancellation decisions?

Sea conditions can of course have an impact — the final decision is always made by the harbor master. If, on the day of the trip, we’re informed that we cannot go out, we’ll offer you an alternative date or a full refund.

[вопрос] - What specific weather conditions (rain, storm, wind, waves) lead to tour cancellation?

Sea conditions or heavy rain can of course have an impact — the final decision is always made by the harbor master. If, on the day of the trip, we’re informed that we cannot go out, we’ll offer you an alternative date or a full refund.

[вопрос] - Does the tour operate during rain or drizzle?

Heavy rain can of course have an impact — the final decision is always made by the harbor master. If, on the day of the trip, we’re informed that we cannot go out, we’ll offer you an alternative date or a full refund.

[вопрос] - Is it safe to snorkel/travel in rainy, stormy, or windy weather?

Rain usually doesn’t affect snorkeling, but if the weather conditions aren’t suitable for snorkeling, our guides may adjust the itinerary to ensure the best and safest experience.

[вопрос] - How often are tours cancelled due to bad weather?

Bad weather can of course have an impact — the final decision is always made by the harbor master. If, on the day of the trip, we’re informed that we cannot go out, we’ll offer you an alternative date or a full refund. The weather in Bali can be unpredictable, but cancellations due to weather are very rare.

[вопрос] - What are the chances of seeing wildlife (e.g., mantas) based on recent sightings and conditions?

The chances of seeing mantas are always very high, but unfortunately, we can’t guarantee their appearance 100% on any given day, as they are wild marine creatures.

[вопрос] - Is the boat ride to Nusa Penida typically bumpy or rough?

It depends on the weather conditions on the day of the tour, which unfortunately can’t be predicted in advance. The ride might be a bit bumpy, but it’s still safe.

[вопрос] - Is water visibility clear for snorkeling?

Water visibility is totally clear for snorkeling around Nusa Penida and Lembongan!

[вопрос] - Is the water temperature cold?

Water temperature depends on the season, but it’s generally not cold and perfect for snorkeling :)
