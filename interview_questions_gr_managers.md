# Interview Questions — Guest Relations Managers
## (Hardi, Edward, Yuda, Mario, Mika)
## Bluuu Tours — BLUUU OS Project

---

## Why We're Doing This

We're building **BLUUU OS** — an AI-powered operations system for Bluuu Tours. The goal is simple: automate the tasks that don't need a human, so you can spend your energy on the things that actually require your skill and judgment.

Before we build anything, we need to understand exactly how you work right now — the real process, not the official one. There are no wrong answers. The more specific and honest you are, the better the system we can build for you.

**This interview takes 20–30 minutes.** Record a voice note or write your answers — whatever is easiest.

Everything you share stays within the team.

---

## Questions

### Your Role
1. Describe your job in your own words. What are you responsible for?
2. Walk me through a typical day — from when you start to when you finish.
3. What percentage of your day is spent on WhatsApp vs. Odoo vs. other tools?

### Handling Guest Inquiries
4. When a new guest inquiry comes in (WhatsApp, OTA, website), what's the first thing you do?
5. What are the top 5 questions guests ask most often?
6. Are there questions that are hard to answer because you don't have the information easily? What are they?
7. How do you check availability when a guest asks for a specific date?
8. How long does it usually take from first guest message to confirmed booking?

### Booking Process
9. Walk me through the full booking process — from the moment a guest says "I want to book" to the moment the booking is confirmed. Every step.
10. What information do you need from the guest to complete a booking?
11. How do you send the booking confirmation? What does it include?
12. What happens if the guest doesn't pay on time? What's the follow-up process?
13. What happens if a guest books through Viator/Klook but then contacts you directly with a question?

### Problems & Escalation
14. What are the most common complaints guests have before their trip?
15. When do you escalate to Dima? What kinds of situations?
16. Have you ever made a mistake with a booking (wrong date, wrong boat, wrong price)? What happened?
17. What is the most stressful part of your job?

### Cancellations & Changes
18. How do you handle a cancellation? What is the process step by step?
19. How do you handle a date change request?
20. What's the refund policy and how do you communicate it to guests?

### Weather & Emergencies
21. Who tells you when a tour is cancelled due to weather? How do you find out?
22. What do you say to guests when a tour is cancelled? Is there a script?
23. How many guests do you typically need to notify in a weather cancellation? How long does it take?

### Tools & Automation
24. What do you copy-paste or repeat every single day?
25. Is there information you wish you had automatically — without having to ask someone?
26. If one thing in your job could be automated, what would it be?
27. What would make your job significantly easier or less stressful?
