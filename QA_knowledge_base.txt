1. Booking & Availability
Q: How can I book a tour with Bluuu Tours?
A: You can book any tour directly on our website. The process is quick and easy — just choose your tour, select the date, and complete the payment. You can pay a 50% deposit to confirm, and settle the remainder at our office before the trip.
Q: Can I book via WhatsApp, email, or phone?
A: Yes, you can reach us on WhatsApp, by email, or directly through our website. You can find our contact details on the website. We recommend booking directly on the website to avoid any extra fees.
Q: Do I need to book in advance?
A: Yes, advance booking is required with a 50% deposit to confirm your spot. You can book any time up until 8:30 PM the evening before the tour, as long as there is availability.
Q: Can I book last-minute or for tomorrow?
A: Yes! You can book a tour for the next day as long as you do so before 8:30 PM Bali time and spots are still available.
Q: Can I book through TripAdvisor, Klook, or other platforms?
A: Yes! Our tours are available on most major booking platforms including TripAdvisor, Klook, Viator, GetYourGuide, and Airbnb. However, booking directly on our website is recommended to avoid extra commissions or fees.
Q: How far in advance must I book?
A: You can book up until 8:30 PM the day before the tour, as long as there is availability. For popular dates, we recommend booking earlier.
Q: What information do I need to provide when booking?
A: You'll need to complete our online web check-in, providing the full name, age, and nationality of each guest. These are mandatory requirements from the harbor master for every trip.
Q: Is there a minimum number of guests required to book?
A: The minimum is just one guest for shared tours. Private tours are priced per boat, so you can book for just your group. However, shared tours may be cancelled if the minimum number of travelers is not met, in which case we'll offer an alternative date or full refund.
Q: Can I hold a reservation without paying?
A: Unfortunately, we cannot confirm a booking without a deposit. A minimum 50% deposit is required to secure your date.
Q: Can I add more people to my booking after it's confirmed?
A: Yes, you can modify the number of guests in your personal account, up to 24 hours before the tour departure. For shared tours, additions are subject to availability.
Q: Can I book a private tour just for my group?
A: Absolutely! We have a wide selection of private boats exclusively for your group. Visit our website to see available options and pricing.
Q: Is it better to book on your website or on a third-party platform?
A: It's always better to book directly on our website to avoid additional or hidden fees charged by online booking platforms.
Q: Do you operate tours every day?
A: Our tours run every day without breaks, except on major Balinese ceremonies and Indonesian public holidays. Availability for those dates is blocked in advance.
Q: What are your business/working hours?
A: We operate every day from 9 AM to 6 PM Bali time (GMT+8).
Q: How do I get booking confirmation?
A: You will receive a booking confirmation email as soon as your payment is processed, within just a few minutes. If you don't receive it, please check your spam folder or contact us immediately.
Q: Can I view boat photos or documents before booking?
A: You can view high-quality photos of all our boats on our website. Unfortunately, we cannot provide official boat documents for security reasons, and boats may be out on tours daily.
Q: Can I book a driver for hotel transfers separately?
A: You can add hotel transfers during the tour booking process on our website. We don't offer standalone transfer bookings at this time.
Q: Is booking online secure?
A: Yes, 100% secure. All payments are processed through our secure payment platform.
Q: What are the next steps after booking?
A: You'll receive a confirmation email and a link to register your personal account. Your guide will message you the day before the trip. 
Q: Is there an availability check for specific dates?
A: Yes — you can check availability and pricing for any date directly on our website by entering your date and group size.
Q: Can I book from Nusa Penida rather than Bali?
A: If you'd like to join a tour starting from Nusa Penida rather than Bali, only private tour options are available. Our shared tours depart from Bali only.
Q: Can I book only specific services like photography or drone filming?
A: You can book available add-ons (photographer, drone pilot, transfers, etc.) during the tour booking process or later through your personal account on our website.

3. Cancellations, Refunds & Modifications
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: You can cancel and receive a full refund if you cancel at least 24 hours before the tour's scheduled start time. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before departure are non-refundable, regardless of the reason (illness, wrong booking date, etc.).
Q: Can I get a full refund?
A: Yes, a full refund is available if you cancel at least 24 hours before the tour. Refunds are processed minus any fees charged by the payment platform.
Q: Can I reschedule my booking?
A: Yes, you can change your tour date up to 24 hours before departure, subject to availability and cancellation policy. Changes can be made in your personal account on our website, too.
Q: What happens if I cancel less than 24 hours before the tour?
A: No refund or rescheduling is possible for cancellations made less than 24 hours before departure, for any reason — including illness, incorrect booking date, or inability to attend.
Q: Can I get a refund if I'm ill?
A: Illness-related cancellations are only eligible for a refund if the cancellation is made at least 24 hours before departure. If you cancel within 24 hours due to illness, no refund is possible.
Q: What if the tour is cancelled due to bad weather?
A: If the Port Authority or harbor master cancels the tour due to unfavorable conditions (high waves, strong winds), we will offer you a full refund or a free reschedule to another date.
Q: How do I cancel my booking?
A: If you booked on our website, you can cancel through your personal account at least 24 hours before the tour. For bookings through third-party platforms, please contact that platform's support team directly.
Q: How long does a refund take?
A: We process refunds immediately upon cancellation. It may take a few days for funds to appear in your account, depending on your bank.
Q: How will my refund be processed?
A: Refunds are issued through the same payment method used for booking (card, PayPal, etc.). We will notify you once the refund has been initiated.
Q: Is the transfer (pickup/drop-off) refundable if the tour is cancelled?
A: The car transfer service is non-refundable if you have already used it and the tour is subsequently cancelled due to weather or other reasons.
Q: What if I decide to leave the tour early?
A: If you leave a shared tour early, our guide will help you arrange a public speedboat back to Bali (at your own expense). For private tours, the boat can return at your request. No refund is possible for early departure.
Q: Can I change the number of guests in my booking?
A: Yes, you can change the guest count in your personal account up to 24 hours before departure. Changes within 24 hours cannot be guaranteed.
Q: What is the policy if the minimum number of travelers is not met?
A: If a shared tour is cancelled due to insufficient travelers, we'll offer you an alternative date or a full refund.
Q: Is the deposit refundable?
A: Yes, the deposit is fully refundable if you cancel at least 24 hours before the tour start time.
Q: Can I transfer my booking to someone else?
A: Please contact us to discuss any changes to guest names or transferring a booking.
Q: Will I get a confirmation email after cancellation?
A: We will notify you as soon as the refund and cancellation have been processed.
Q: What if I booked through TripAdvisor and need to cancel?
A: For bookings made through third-party platforms (TripAdvisor, Klook, Viator, Airbnb, etc.), all changes and cancellations must be managed through that platform's customer support.
Q: Can I cancel if there are no manta rays sighted?
A: Manta ray sightings are not guaranteed as they are wild animals. No refund is provided if mantas are not seen, as this is beyond our control. We always visit the best spots and make every effort to find them.
4. Itinerary & Activities
Q: What is the standard tour itinerary?
A: For classic shared tours, our itinerary includes: approximate 6:30 AM hotel pickup (optional), 7:30 AM briefing at our office, 8:00 AM boat departure, snorkeling at Bali Hai, SD Point, and Wall Point, lunch at a restaurant on Nusa Penida, Kelingking Cliff land tour, snorkeling with Manta Rays (Manta Point or Manta Bay), 4:00–4:30 PM return to Bali, approximate 5:30-6:00 PM hotel drop-off (optional). For premium shared tours, our itinerary includes: approximate 7:00 AM hotel pickup (optional), 8:00 AM briefing at our office, 8:30 AM boat departure, snorkeling at Bali Hai, SD Point, and Wall Point, lunch at a restaurant on Nusa Penida, Kelingking Cliff land tour, snorkeling with Manta Rays (Manta Point or Manta Bay), 5:30 PM return to Bali, approximate 6:30 PM hotel drop-off (optional). For private tours, our itinerary includes: approximate 7:30 AM hotel pickup (optional), 8:30 AM briefing at our office, 9:00-9:30 AM boat departure, snorkeling at Bali Hai, SD Point, and Wall Point, lunch at a restaurant on Nusa Penida/Lembongan, Kelingking Cliff land tour, snorkeling with Manta Rays (Manta Point or Manta Bay), 5:00-5:30 PM return to Bali, approximate 6:00-06:30 PM hotel drop-off (optional). 
Q: How long is the tour?
A: The tour is approximately 8–9 hours long, depending on the tour type you choose.
Q: What time does the boat depart?
A: Shared tours depart at 8:00 AM, premium shared tours at 8:30 AM, and private tours typically depart between 9:00–10:00 AM depending on the boat. Check specific itineraries on our website.
Q: What time do we return?
A: Shared tours return to Serangan Harbor around 4:30–5:30 PM, with hotel drop-off approximately by 5:30-6:30 PM. Return times for private tours depend on the chosen itinerary.
Q: Can the itinerary be customized?
A: On shared tours, the itinerary is fixed and cannot be changed. On private tours, you can make some adjustments — such as adding/removing stops, changing timing, or skipping the land tour. Choose your best itinerary during the booking process or contact us to discuss your preferences.
Q: Can we start later than the standard departure time?
A: On private tours, a later departure is possible (11:30 AM as latest), but it may mean visiting fewer snorkeling spots, as all boats must return to Bali by 6:00 PM. Shared tours have fixed departure times.
Q: Is there a sunset or evening tour available?
A: We do not offer sunset or evening tours, as all boats must return to the port no later than 6:00 PM. Evening tours are not allowed due to safety regulations.
Q: Is an early morning departure available?
A: Early morning departures earlier than 8:00 AM are not possible due to port operating hours.
Q: Is there a land tour included?
A: On shared tours, a land tour (Kelingking Cliff by car) is included. On private tours, you can choose an itinerary with or without a land tour, or opt for a full day land tour around Nusa Penida.
Q: Can we do the tour without snorkeling?
A: On private tours, you can skip snorkeling and opt for a full land tour around Nusa Penida instead. Please check available itinerary options on our website.
Q: Can we visit both east and west Nusa Penida?
A: Yes! We offer a full-day land tour of both east and west Nusa Penida. Note that snorkeling is not included in this tour due to time constraints.
Q: Is it possible to extend the tour duration?
A: On private tours, you can add an optional one-hour extension to relax on the boat near the harbor. Major extensions may not be possible due to port curfew.
Q: What happens if it rains on the day of the tour?
A: If the harbor master does not allow departure due to heavy rain or unsafe conditions, we'll offer a full refund or alternative date. If rain occurs during the tour, the captain may adjust the itinerary for safety, and we'll offer an alternative route — no refund is issued for itinerary changes due to weather.
Q: Can we add Kelingking Cliff to a private tour?
A: Yes! You can add a land tour to Kelingking Cliff during the booking process or through your personal account.
Q: Can we hike down to Kelingking Beach?
A: Going down to Kelingking Beach is currently not allowed as part of our tours — it is considered dangerous, is not covered by our insurance, and takes too much time.
Q: Can we visit Diamond Beach?
A: Yes! Diamond Beach is available on the East Nusa Penida land tour option. Choose this itinerary during booking.
Q: Can we visit Broken Beach and Angel Billabong?
A: Yes! Broken Beach and Angel Billabong are included in some private tour itineraries. You can add these stops during booking.
Q: How many snorkeling spots are included?
A: The standard tour includes 4 snorkeling spots: Bali Hai, SD Point, Wall Point, and either Manta Bay or Manta Point (whichever has a higher chance of manta sightings that day).
Q: How long do we spend at each snorkeling spot?
A: We spend approximately 30 minutes at each snorkeling spot.
Q: What is Manta Bay vs Manta Point — which is better?
A: Both are excellent spots for swimming with manta rays. We visit Manta Bay first — if mantas aren't there, we head to Manta Point. The choice depends on where manta rays are most likely to appear on the day.
Q: What other activities besides snorkeling are available?
A: Besides snorkeling, we offer land tours (Kelingking Cliff, Diamond Beach, Broken Beach, Tree Houses), scuba diving tours, and relaxing cruises on the boat.
Q: Is canoeing, kayaking, or paddleboarding available?
A: Yes, you can add a canoe, banana boat or donut, flying fish or sea walker as an add-on for your private tour, during the booking process.
Q: Is there a half-day tour option?
A: We do not offer traditional half-day tours to Nusa Penida due to travel time. All our tours are approximately 8–9 hours to ensure you experience everything Nusa Penida has to offer.
Q: Can we propose on the boat? Can you set up a special surprise?
A: Absolutely! A boat trip is a perfect setting for a proposal. Let us know what you need and we'll help make it unforgettable — special setup, timing, or added surprises.
Q: Can we have more time at lunch?
A: On private tours, you can spend more time at the restaurant, but this will reduce the number of snorkeling spots visited. On shared tours, the lunch duration is fixed at approximately one hour.
Q: What is the land tour like?
A: Our driver picks you up by car and takes you to several stunning viewpoints on Nusa Penida. The exact stops depend on your chosen itinerary — typically including Kelingking Cliff, and optionally Diamond Beach, Broken Beach, Angel Billabong, and Tree Houses.
Q: Is there walking involved in the tour?
A: On the standard itinerary, there's a short walk from the parking area at Kelingking Cliff. Full land tours involve short walks at each attraction.
Q: What is the condition of the roads on Nusa Penida?
A: Road conditions vary and some areas are rough. However, we can reach almost every point on the island comfortably in our cars.

A: On a shared tour, you share the boat with other groups (maximum 14 guests total). On a private tour, the entire boat is reserved exclusively for your group, and you have more flexibility to adjust the itinerary, bring alcohol, add extras, and customize the experience.
Q: How many people are on a shared boat?
A: The maximum number of guests on a shared tour is 14 people.
Q: What is the maximum capacity for private tours?
A: The capacity for private tours depends on the boat you choose. Options range from 8-person boats up to large yachts for 25+ guests. Check boat options on our website.
Q: Can I upgrade from shared to private?
A: Yes, you can upgrade through your personal account or by contacting our team. Upgrades are subject to availability.
Q: What is a Premium Shared Tour?
A: Our premium shared tours feature higher-class boats, a professional photographer on board, complimentary prosecco (3 bottles per boat), welcome drinks, hoodie towels, and a secret spot.
Q: What is the difference between Standard Shared and Premium Shared?
A: The classic shared tour is on a classic speedboat with up to 14 guests and standard inclusions, such as underwater GoPro footage, drinking water, snorkeling equipment, towels, certified guides, lunch at Amarta Penida restaurant, land tour and health insurance; while The Premium Shared additionally features a higher-class boat, professional photographer, prosecco, lunch at La Rossa restaurant, hoodie towels, and an extra secret spot.
Q: Is a private tour just for our group?
A: Yes, on a private tour the boat is reserved exclusively for your group — no other guests.
Q: Can a private tour accommodate a large group?
A: Yes! We have boats suitable for groups of all sizes. Please contact us to discuss the best boat for your group size.
Q: What is the Classic Speedboat?
A: This is our 12-meter boat accommodating up to 14 guests — perfect for family and friends trips.
Q: What is the "Small Sweetheart" boat?
A: A beautiful 10-meter boat accommodating up to 8 guests, ideal for small groups.
Q: Can I request a specific boat?
A: Yes, you can select your preferred boat during the booking process on our website. Each boat has photos and descriptions to help you choose.
