The Spots

Best snorkeling spots

All 30 minutes from Serangan Harbor. Crystal-clear water, vibrant reefs, and marine life every day.

🏖️

Bali Hai

Vibrant coral gardens and tropical fish. Perfect for beginners and families.

🧗

Wall Point

Dramatic underwater wall with pelagic species. A favorite for experienced snorkelers.

⭐

SD Point

Pristine reefs and abundant marine life. Excellent visibility, perfect for all levels.

🦈

Manta Bay/Point

Manta rays seen almost every day. One of the world's best manta ray encounters.

Your Day

What your day looks like

A smooth full-day flow — snorkeling, lunch, land highlights, and manta time.

Morning
Hotel Pickup & Cruise
Your driver arrives at the hotel. Board the yacht at Serangan Harbor and cruise to Nusa Penida with your expert guide.
Morning
Cruise & Snorkel
Visit curated snorkel spots with calm, crystal-clear waters. Your crew guides you to the best reefs and marine life.
Midday
Lunch & Relax
Lunch with panoramic ocean views and time to refresh. Recharge before the second half of the day.
Afternoon
Land Highlights
Short land stops for iconic viewpoints — Kelingking Cliff, Crystal Bay, and photo moments at every turn.
Late Afternoon
Swim with Mantas
Manta ray swim (conditions permitting), guided by the crew in one of the world's best manta spots.
Sunset
Back to Bali
Return crossing with sunset views. Elite guests enjoy prosecco on the cruise back. Hotel drop-off included.
