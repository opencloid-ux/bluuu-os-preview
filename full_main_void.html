The Spots

Best snorkeling spots

All 30 minutes from Serangan Harbor. Crystal-clear water, vibrant reefs, and marine life every day.

Bali Hai

Vibrant coral gardens and tropical fish. Perfect for beginners and families.

Wall Point

Dramatic underwater wall with pelagic species. A favorite for experienced snorkelers.

SD Point

Pristine reefs and abundant marine life. Excellent visibility, perfect for all levels.

Manta Bay/Point

Manta rays seen almost every day. One of the world's best manta ray encounters.

Marine Life

Wildlife you'll encounter

Bali's underwater world is extraordinary. Here's what our guests see most often.

Manta Rays

Seen almost every day. Majestic, graceful — the highlight of every trip.

Sea Turtles

Gentle giants gliding through the reef. Very common sightings year-round.

Dolphins

Playful pod encounters on the boat ride. Pure magic when they appear.

Tropical Fish

Hundreds of colorful species in coral gardens that come alive underwater.

Your Day

What your day looks like

A smooth full-day flow — snorkeling, lunch, land highlights, and manta time.

☕
1
Early Morning
Bluuu Lounge — before you even board
Start your day at our private lounge in Serangan. Relax, grab a coffee, and meet your guide before the boat departs.
  • Coffee, tea & fresh orange juice
  • Fresh bakery
  • Safety briefing in the lounge — calm, not rushed
🚢
2
Morning
Board from our private pier
No beach walk, no dinghy, no low-tide odyssey. Our dedicated pier in Serangan lets you walk directly onto the boat — dry, easy, and dignified.
  • No 300–500 m ocean walk at low tide
  • No shuttle dinghy to reach your boat
  • Safer for kids, families & seniors
🤿
3
Morning
Into the water — first snorkel
Crystal-clear protected reefs. Your guide enters with you. Sea turtles, colourful coral, and the first rush of "wait, is that a manta?" energy.
  • Full snorkel gear included
  • Guide in water with every group
  • Sea turtles seen on most dives
🍽️
4
Midday ★ Premium & Private
Lunch at La Rossa Beach Club
Premium and Private guests eat at La Rossa — a beachfront restaurant with ocean views, ice-cold drinks, and real food. Not a buffet on the boat.
  • Beachfront table with ocean view
  • Full menu — not a set meal
  • Vegetarian options available
  • Standard guests: catered lunch onboard
🦈
5
Afternoon
Manta Bay — the main event
We time our arrival at Manta Bay in the afternoon when fewer boats are out and conditions are calmer. ~90% sighting rate. Wingspans up to 5 metres.
  • ~90% sighting rate on our tours
  • Afternoon slot — fewer crowds
  • If no mantas: Crystal Bay reef + $15 credit/person
🥂
6
Sunset ★ Premium & Private
Secret spot — golden hour & Prosecco
Our Premium and Private guests end the day at a hidden spot. Chilled Prosecco, golden light, and that feeling you can't quite describe to people back home.
  • Secret location — not on maps
  • Chilled Prosecco served onboard
  • Best photo moment of the whole trip
  • Standard guests: sunset views on cruise back
🌙
7
Evening
Hotel drop-off — door to door
Back at Serangan pier, your driver takes you straight to your hotel. No taxis, no haggling, no figuring it out after a full day.
  • Private driver, same car as pickup
  • Drop-off directly at your hotel
  • Typical return: 17:30–18:30
